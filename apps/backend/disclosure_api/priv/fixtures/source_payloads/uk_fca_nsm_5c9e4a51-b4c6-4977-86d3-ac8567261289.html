Scheme of Arrangement Becomes Effective

RNS Number: 0943B

British Land Co PLC

20 April 2026

NOT FOR RELEASE, PUBLICATION OR DISTRIBUTION, IN WHOLE OR IN PART, IN OR INTO ANY RESTRICTED JURISDICTION.

BRITISH LAND COMPANY PUBLIC LIMITED COMPANY(THE) announces that the scheme of arrangement relating to its recommended cash and share offer for Life Science REIT PLC has become effective in accordance with its terms.

The Court Order sanctioning the Scheme has been delivered to the Registrar of Companies and the Scheme has therefore become effective. The acquisition is being implemented by means of a Court-sanctioned scheme of arrangement under Part 26 of the Companies Act 2006.

The cancellation of admission to trading of Life Science REIT PLC shares is expected to occur after the effective time in accordance with the published timetable.

This announcement is made through Regulatory News Services (RNS) and is stored on the FCA National Storage Mechanism.
